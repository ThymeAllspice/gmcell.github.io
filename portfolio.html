About

Hello! My name is Grace and I am currently working to get my Master's degree under the guidance of Dr. Warren in Geology at the University of Delaware. I got my Bachelor's degree at the University of Minnesota Duluth (also in Geology) and grew up in Eau Claire, WI. My area of study focuses on peridotites formed at mid-ocean ridges and the following deformation that they experience at transform faults. In my free time, I like to hike, climb, and run for exercise. I draw, play my bass guitar, and read any good fantasy or sci-fi book with my birds Thyme and Allspice.
